Tipps and Links

There are a lot of options how you can improve your privacy on the web. Most of them just need a few click to install and afterward you barly notice them. Sometimes it is important to read the AGBs but who has time or motivation to do that anyway, right?

There are different options how you can find out if the AGB is ok or not. I use TOSDR. This have done for me a good job, but of course not every website is in there. After installing the plugin I just have to look in the browsers bar to see the rating and I have an idea how good this website is in terms of privacy.

Google is often called as one of the big companies collecting our data about tied with Facebook. There is a simple alternative to Google. Its called duckduckgo.com, I start to use it and I got less adds (especially no hidden adds), found more excect what I was looking for and I was more private then ever. Our school recommends either duckduckgo or startpage.com which is google without the tracking and profiling.
